About FutureForge

FutureForge empowers teens, parents, and counselors to confidently navigate post-secondary pathways with personalized guidance, mentorship, and accessible resources—all in one engaging app.

We built FutureForge to address the widespread anxiety and uncertainty teens face when planning life after high school, especially those lacking access to resources and mentorship. Existing tools are often limited to school systems, leaving many students unsupported.

Our research showed strong demand for a comprehensive, easy-to-use app that combines career exploration, financial aid, and wellness in one place. By including parents and counselors, we strengthen the support network for students. Ultimately, FutureForge aims to level the playing field and help every young person build a confident, informed future.

Key Features

Career Explorer

Discover career paths aligned with your interests, strengths, and goals. Explore detailed information about educational requirements, salary expectations, and job growth forecasts.

Mentor Connect

Connect with experienced professionals and educators who can provide guidance and insights on your chosen path. Schedule virtual meetings and get personalized advice.

Smart Aid Finder

Navigate financial aid options with personalized recommendations for scholarships, grants, and other funding opportunities based on your profile and preferences.

Skill Builders

Develop essential skills through interactive modules and resources designed to prepare you for college, career, and life success. Track your progress and earn achievement badges.

Mindfulness & Wellness

Manage stress and maintain well-being with guided mindfulness practices, emotion tracking, and wellness resources designed for busy students navigating important life decisions.
